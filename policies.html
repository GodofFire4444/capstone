Student Honour Code


Under the guidance of IIT Gandhinagar’s academic code of conduct, the CSE discipline is committed to upholding and maintaining the highest standards of integrity and probity in all aspects of teaching, education and research. In this way, it is the responsibility of every student to refrain from committing or assisting in all forms of academic dishonesty, fraud and deceit. Students violating this honor code are liable to stern disciplinary action.
